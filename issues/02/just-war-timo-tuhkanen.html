Timo Tuhkanen (b. 1983) is a poet, artist, and composer. His work encompasses material and immaterial art as he deals with new contexts, concepts and possibilities between classical music and contemporary art. His work has been presented in several Museums, institutions and alternative spaces in Europe, Russia and United States. He is at present a doctoral candidate at the University of Leeds.
