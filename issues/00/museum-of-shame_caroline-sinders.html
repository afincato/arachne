Museum of Shame

by Caroline Sinders

About: These are a combinations of tweets documenting IRL cat calls I have received. Other documents in the museum are items like emails, linkedin messages, etc of a very flirtatious nature that were received unsolicited and akin to digital catcalling. Some items are reddit posts commenting on the nature of my looks, and my work, in a misogynistic tone.The “Museum of Shame” is a digital museum documenting the different kinds of unsolicited and unwanted interactions that I have received from men on the internet and in ’real life.
