Donations

Arachne is a self-sustained project (we cover the cost implied in running it), made out of personal engagement with the topics discussed in each issue, and with the intent to thoroughly analyse also the implications of immaterial labour on a larger scale.

For these we pay each person contributing to the zine.

Your donation is very helpful for us to keep working on the project and to expand it onto other formats and media. At the same time, it would let us keep doing the work necessary to look and apply for grants, in order to turn the project self-sufficient.

Thank you!
